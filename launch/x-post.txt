Paperjam: chaos engineering for OCR.

It mutates a document into hostile variants like glare, highlights, stamps, blur, copier streaks, and messaging-app compression, then runs local OCR and shows which failure mode actually breaks the page.

The useful shift in Document AI right now is not just GLM-OCR vs OpenOCR vs Tesseract vs LlamaParse.
It’s whether your stack survives ugly real documents *after* the model swap.

Recent SAP + Stanford signal: stronger multimodal stacks still fail in specific document ways.
So I built a local robustness harness, not another parser.

Repo: https://github.com/techwithaijack-beep/paperjam
